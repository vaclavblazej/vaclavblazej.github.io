Scientific poster for poster session

It is quite scary if have to prepare a poster for a conference. The main idea is to transfer the information about your research af efficiently as possible. I found a great video by Mike Morrison ↗ talking about the poster session difficulties. I think this is mainly applicable to big conferences.

Well, I used this format on a quite small and crowded poster session (15 posters in total) and it didn’t go particularly well. The paper format was A2 and the sidebars are quite small, but I have my suspicion which is not connected to the format. Simply put, there were too many people to too small of an area. Some people flourish in such situations, some people don’t, that all.
