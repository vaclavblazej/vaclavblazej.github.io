Science

The science process goes from posing a problem, solving it, writing it down, and finally, publishing the result. This page is intended to help with all of these parts. The main focus is on Theoretical Computer Science (TCS) so its usefulness may be limited to other fields.

Systems

There are several systems that aggregate information about scientific research.

  • Google Scholar ↗ – very broad automatic aggregator of many things
  • DBLP ↗ – a reasonable (mainly automatic) database of TCS publications
  • Scopus ↗ –
  • Web of Science ↗ – slow to propagate the information, taken more seriously

Then, there are evaluations.

  • CORE ↗ – Australia based conference evaluation site.
  • Conference Ranks ↗ – Aggregate of older conference rankings (from ERA, Qualis, and MSAR)
