About

Welcome to my corner of the net! I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Warwick ↗ supervised by M. S. Ramanujan ↗. I am interested in parameterized complexity with focus on structural parameterization.

On this website you may find my research and publications, events and ideas, and compiled materials.

  • Mar 2023 to now – postdoc at Department of Computer Science ↗ of the University of Warwick ↗, DIMAP ↗ working with Ramanujan Sridharan ↗
  • Sep 2022 to Mar 2023 – postdoc at GGOAT ↗ research group at Faculty of Information Technology ↗ (FIT) at Czech technical university in Prague (CTU)
  • untill Sep 2022 – Ph.D. student at FIT CTU ↗ under the supervision of Tomáš Valla ↗
